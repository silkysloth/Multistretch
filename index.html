Our Story

About
Multi Stretch

Who We Are

Your Expert in
Packaging Solutions

At Multi Stretch, we've built our reputation on delivering superior packaging solutions since our inception in 2014. We are a focused manufacturer of high-performance pallet wrap and a trusted supplier of secondary packaging products required by our customer base.

Our Mission — to empower businesses with high-quality, innovative packaging materials that enhance efficiency and sustainability.

Our Vision — to be the foremost provider of customised pallet wrap packaging solutions in Southern Africa, continuously pushing the boundaries of material technology and service excellence.

03

Our Journey

Milestones

2014

Founded in Alrode, Johannesburg

Multi Stretch was established as a focused manufacturer of high-performance pallet wrap, operating from our Alrode facility south of Johannesburg.

2016

Expanded Product Range

Grew beyond pallet wrap to include bubble wrap, refuse bags, general purpose bags, adhesive tape and consumables — becoming a full-service packaging supplier.

2019

Zero-Wastage Manufacturing

Installed in-line scrap rework facility, achieving our goal of zero external wastage and cementing our commitment to sustainable manufacturing.

2022

Africa Export Growth

Expanded distribution into Botswana, Zimbabwe, Lesotho and Zambia, establishing Multi Stretch as a Southern Africa packaging leader.

2024+

Technology Polymer Innovation

Adopted advanced LLDPE Metallocene technology polymers to meet customer auto wrap machine requirements and enhance product performance.
